My Favorite Hobby

Reading is the process of taking in the sense or meaning of symbols, often specifically those of a written language, by means of sight or touch. Reading is the process of looking at a series of written symbols and getting meaning from them. When we read, we use our eyes to receive written symbols (letters, punctuation marks and spaces) and we use our brain to convert them into words, sentences and paragraphs that communicate something to us.
